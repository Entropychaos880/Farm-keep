Farm Keep ☕ 🇰🇪
Farm Keep is an AI-powered agricultural management platform designed specifically for Kenyan coffee farmers. It combines financial tracking with an AI Agronomist to help farmers manage expenses, diagnose crop diseases, and receive real-time treatment recommendations.

🚀 Features
AI Doctor: Real-time crop disease diagnosis using Gemini 1.5 Flash and Llama 3.3 70B.

Persistent Chat: AI conversations are saved to MongoDB, allowing farmers to switch devices without losing history.

Financial Tracking: Log income and expenses easily with automatic AI categorisation.

Localized Advice: Recommendations for fungicides and treatments available in the Kenyan market.

Farm Diary: A digital timeline to track farm activities and observations.

🛠️ Tech Stack
Frontend: React.js, Tailwind CSS, Lucide Icons.

Backend: Node.js, Express.js.

Database: MongoDB Atlas (Mongoose).

AI Models: Google Gemini 1.5 & Groq (Llama 3.3).
